About me


My name is Sylwia Aderek. I was born in Warsaw, Poland. After finishing high-school I spent a gap year (x2) in the UK. In 2014 I came back to Poland to start studies. In 2018 I graduated from Tourism and Recreation Faculty of Jozef Pilsudski University of Physical Education in Warsaw.

Because of the nature of my studies I was not able to stay in one place. I took part in Erasmus+ Exchanges. I lived in Portugal and Greece which are now one of my favourite travel destinations.
